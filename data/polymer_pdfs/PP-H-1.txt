Notes

Other notes

 Clarified polypropylene resins are 'nucleated', meaning that a crystal nucleation additive has been mixed into the resin. The
 additives clarify the material by increasing the number and reducing the size of crystallites in the material. Crystallites beco
 small that they no longer scatter visible light, thereby lowering haze in the material from >50% to <20%.

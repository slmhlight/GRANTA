UdelŽ Polysulfone is an amorphous high performance thermoplastic offering excellent mechanical,

electrical, and improved chemical resistance relative to polycarbonate. Polysulfone's properties

remain relatively consistent over a broad temperature range of -148°F (-100°C) to +300°F (+149°C).

Note: Not all UdelŽ shapes are certified to USP Class VI. If your application requires
certification to USP Class-VI, it must be specified at the time of quotation and before order
placement. Some UdelŽgrades are also offered in ISO 10993 compliant material, which must also
be specified at the time of quotation and before order placement.

Key Properties

   Excellent Radiation Stability
   High Chemical Resistance to Acidic and Salt Solutions, and Good Resistance to Detergents, Hot
   Water, and Steam
   Hydrolysis Resistance for Continuous Use in Hot Water and Steam at Temperatures Up To 300°F
   (149°C)
   Low Ionic Impurity Levels
